AMS Tools of the Trade

Welcome to the Tools of the Trade website. This website is an online compilation of resources provided by past speakers for the Tools of the Trade Session at the AMS Student Conference. Here you will find lots of information about the "tools" (i.e. programming languages and software tools) that you need to succeed in our "trade".
